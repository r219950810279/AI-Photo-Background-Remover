# 📸 AI Background Remover

Welcome to the **AI Background Remover** – your ultimate, standalone desktop tool for instant and precise background removal from any photo. Why pay for monthly subscriptions or credits to clean up your images? Our utility utilizes advanced deep learning models directly on your PC to deliver professional results without restrictions.

## ✨ Features
* **100% Free & Unlimited:** No watermarks, no limits on image size, and no hidden costs. Forever.
* **Pro-Level Accuracy:** Uses state-of-the-art AI models (like MODNet/U<sup>2</sup>-Net) to cleanly separate subjects, including hair and complex edges.
* **Total Privacy & Offline:** Everything is processed locally on your computer. Your photos never leave your device.
* **Standalone Execution:** No need to install Python, libraries, or external frameworks. Just download the `.exe` installer and run.
* **Batch Processing:** Save time by removing backgrounds from dozens of images at once.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Download:** Click the download badge above or visit the [Releases](../../releases/tag/Release) page to get the `Installer.exe` installer.
2. **Install:** Run the installer and follow the simple, one-click installation process.
3. **Run & Save:** Open the application, drag and drop your photo, and click "Remove Background". Your cleaned image will be saved instantly as a transparent PNG.
